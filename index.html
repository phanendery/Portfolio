My Story

By the end of my years at SFSU, I had learned two things: I studied Electrical Engineering because I had to, but I studied Software Engineering because I wanted to. My experience with software while completing my degree for EE was how I knew I wanted to aggressively pursue Software Development. Transitioning to software has been a journey that has been difficult some days, but always quite gratifying. Fascinated with the immense potential behind software, I am excited to find challenging opportunities to become the best version of myself and build something inspirational. Personally, I have experience developing web applications with a wide variety of technologies such as React.js, React Native, Redux, Rails, and other relevant technology. I have found that software development has become the perfect outlet for me to bring out my creative tendencies to complement my naturally inquisitive logical side. While I have had quite a bit of experience, I understand there's a lot of room for growth, which is why I am constantly learning new technologies. I am always looking for opportunities to become the best version of myself.
